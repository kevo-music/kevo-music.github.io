Bio

Short: Kévo crafts intimate, nocturnal pop — music for boys who love boys, tracing the line between quiet chaos and neon calm.

Long: Kévo is not just sound, but a mood. A queer lens on love, longing, and late nights. Each track is a fragment of lived experience — bruises turned into beats, kisses stretched into choruses.
